For Alexandra
Wedding Vows

There were so many days, months, and years where I wondered if I would ever, finally, find the "one". How would I find someone who likes Radiohead? Someone who enjoys spending hours hunting down vintage records, CDs, and cassettes at dingy old stores in New York City? Who can deal with a UFO fanatic's non-stop search for proof that aliens exist? Who can handle some Jersey 'tude and can return it in equal measure? Someone who is always up for an adventure whether it be in Encinitas or a derelict factory in Berlin? And someone who is willing to get a little weird at that factory in Berlin? But most importantly… how would I find someone who checks off ALL of those boxes AND is loved by my friends and family?

Alexandra Brown… I love you for being you. You checked off every single one of those boxes. One of my greatest pleasures is watching you interact with our friends and family. There is a special "something" that you have which very few people do. An ability to connect with people. Not just a surface level connection though. A true, deep, meaningful connection. And your "energy". You have an energy that is efficacious. It affects everyone around you. Without you, every get together, every party, every trip, every day would not be as vibrant. And without you, I would not be complete. You're my gal. I've never doubted it. Never. Our love for each other. Our foundation. Will never crack or falter for the rest of our lives. And the family we create together will be built upon the strength of that foundation, blossoming into the family we always dreamed of having. I cannot wait. Love you forever.

With all of my love, forever
